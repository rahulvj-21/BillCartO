How to Use the BillCartO App

Step.1 --> Firstly create an account on BillCartO app and then login into the application with the credential created.


Step.2 --> Now If you have to create a new bill then click on "Create bill".


Step.3 --> Enter all the details regarding the bill and also about the bill owner.

Step.4 --> If you wanna see all the bills created in an time period, according to name and mobile number, then you can go for the "View all bills".

Step.5 --> You can also customize various settings to check about different bills details , the paid ones and unpaid also.

Step.6 --> Also the already created bills can also be edited using different editing details and manipulate various entries.

Step.7 --> Product statistics can also be checked through the "Product Statistics" features and know about various product sale.
